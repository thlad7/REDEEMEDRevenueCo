Frequently Asked Questions

Do you require long-term contracts?
+
No, we work on a month-to-month model. We believe our results should keep you partnered with us, not a binding legal document. We ask for a 30-day notice prior to stopping campaigns to cleanly wrap up active ad cycles.
Does the monthly price include the ad spend?
+
No. The packages cover our full-service management, content creation, copywriting, setup, and reporting. Your ad budget (paid directly to Google or Facebook) is controlled entirely by you. During our Discovery Call, we'll recommend an optimal budget for your local market size.
What if I already have a website?
+
If you choose our Momentum or Kingdom Builder package, we will run a full technical audit on your current site. If it's performing well, we will optimize it for higher conversions. If it's outdated or slowing you down, we'll build and manage a brand-new custom asset for you as part of the service.
How quickly will I see results?
+
Paid traffic campaigns (Google and Meta Ads) can begin generating leads within the first 14 days of going live. Organic optimization (Google Business Profile and SEO) is a compounding long-term play that usually hits its true momentum within 60 to 90 days.
